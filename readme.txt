This plugin allows you to manage the subscription to centrico, your users will be registered in centrico and will receive your newsletter.

== Description ==

Simplifying the enrollment of new users from your Wordpress site has never been easier!
This plugin allows you to add contacts to Centrico thanks to a custom form that you can place on your website.

Centrico is the software for the Customer Relationship Management that, thanks to this plugin, it can collect data of your site visitors and send them email campaigns and SMS.

But not only that: Centrico simplifies the organization of contacts, manage and share calendars, appointments and activities.
A Web Based Management usable from any device, completely cross platform.
Has a user friendly interface: a simple and intuitive graphic allows you to customize content in a completely autonomous.

Do you not have an account on Centrico? Create one, Centrico is FREE for manage up to 250 contacts and 250 e-mails a day! visit http://www.centrico.it and register.

To have more info about Centrico you can visit our site http://www.centrico.it

== Installation ==

1. In the admin panel of your site in WordPress, select from the menu Plugins > New Plugin, search "Centrico" and click "Install Now".

2. Activate the plugin.

3. Login to your Centrico account from http://console.centrico.it, if you have not yet your account create one of http://www.centrico.it and sign up, it's FREE!

4. From the menu on the left click on "Contact" and then "Lists". Select the list of your interest (you should have only one at the beginning), the one to which they will enroll new contacts from the site, then click on "Info". This will open a pop-up where you will find the list ID at the bottom left. Similarly, clicking on "Groups" and select the group of your interest, you can add new contacts to the group.

5. Enter in the "Enter the list ID" the ID retrieved from the console Centrico; you can also enter the group ID, group ID, or more separated by comma.

6. In the form that will appear in the front-end will have the opportunity to enter the e-mail with its flag to the "Privacy and Policy", you decide whether to show also the "Name" field, the field "Mobile" and credits Centrico.

After you select the options of your interest, you can enter the shortcode [centrico] in pages, posts or into the widget.

Do you need help? Contact us info@centrico.it


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How to display the form in posts, pages? =
By using the shortcode [centrico].

= How to display a form in the widget area, as the sidebar or footer? =
Access from Menu to Appearance > Widgets and add the widget "Centrico Widgets" in a sidebar or click on it.

= Where do I find the list ID and Group ID? =
Sign in to your account from Centrico http://www.centrico.it, if you have not yet your account create one on http://www.centrico.it , it's FREE! From the menu on the left click on "Contact" and then "Lists". Selecting the list of your interest, the one to which they will enroll new contacts from the site, click on "Info". This will open a pop-up where you will find the list ID at the bottom left.

= How can I change the style of the registration form? =
You can use custom CSS to change the style of the registration form.
You can add custom CSS code to your style sheet or (more simply) using a simple plugin like Simple Custom CSS https://wordpress.org/plugins/simple-custom-css/.

Contact us info@centrico.it
